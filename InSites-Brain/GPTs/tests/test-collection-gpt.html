Collection Description

A cross-regional heritage collection spanning four European and Middle Eastern sites, representing industrial, archaeological, medieval, and vernacular heritage typologies. The collection covers roughly two millennia of built heritage — from Roman-era archaeology to 19th-century industrial architecture — across Israel, Austria, and Finland.
